Featured Paper

Sentiment Analysis of Government Policies Using NLP

A comprehensive study on public perception analysis

By Aman Tripathi
2024
Academic Paper

This research explores advanced Natural Language Processing techniques to analyze public sentiment regarding government policies in the Indian context. The study employs machine learning models including BERT transformers and classical NLP methods to extract meaningful insights from social media data and policy documents.

Co-Authors & Collaborators:

Dr. Research Guide Prof. Academic Mentor

Abstract

Understanding public perception is crucial for the successful implementation of government policies. This study employs established Natural Language Processing (NLP) techniques to gauge public sentiment of government policies in Indian context.

Methodology

  • Data Collection from multiple social media platforms
  • Text Preprocessing and Tokenization
  • Sentiment Classification using Deep Learning
  • Trend Analysis and Visualization
1

Key Findings

  • 78% accuracy in sentiment classification
  • Identified 5 major policy sentiment clusters
  • Real-time trend tracking capability
  • Predictive insights for policy outcomes

Conclusions

Our NLP framework provides actionable insights for policymakers and stakeholders to understand and respond to public opinion effectively.
